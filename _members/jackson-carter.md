Jackson Carter (he/him) is a PURE student in the CGK lab since joining in October 2025. Jackson is a current senior at the University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point studying psychology with a neuroscience minor. During his time there, Jackson has worked in the labs of Drs. Jennifer Bray and Heather Molenda-Figueira exploring the ultrastructure of a mouse pituitary gland and hormone-disrupting chemicals and the effects on maternal behavior. He is focused on gaining experience and expertise to pursue a PhD in neuroscience beginning in Fall 2026. Outside of the lab, Jackson enjoys bowling, playing volleyball, spending time with friends and family, and exploring the beauty of Wisconsin seasons.
